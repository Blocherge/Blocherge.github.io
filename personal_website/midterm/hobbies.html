Computer Refurbishing

When I was 12, I had the business idea to buy used computers from companies, refurbish them, and sell them at a profit. I've had the opportunity to work with more than 200 laptops since then, which has given me extensive experience in hardware diagnostics, software installation, and performance optimization. I'm always looking for more opportunities to give used computers a new life and would love to expand this and work with more companies.
